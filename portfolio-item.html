My journey

PhilSA Volleyball

I am the one of the captains of the 2022 PhilSA Volleyball team and have been a member of PhilSA since 2020. One of my favorite memories from PhilSA volleyball was GoodPhil 2022, which is an annual sports tournament held at various universities across Texas consisting of other Filipino student organizations.

Texas A&M Volleyball

I am one of the members of the Texas A&M Men's Club Volleyball team. I was starting B team setter and my team placed 5th at SIVA championships, as well as 16th at NCVF nationals in the d3 league. I am very grateful to have been given the opportunity to play collegiate club volleyball at a competitive level and hope to continue my journey into my junior and senior year.
